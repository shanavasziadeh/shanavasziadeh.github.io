About Shanavas

Where others see hazards, Shanavas Ziadeh sees opportunities to save lives. With five years of elite HSE experience in the UAE, he has become the trusted safety architect for ADNOC contractors, subcontractors, and facilities management organizations across Dubai and Fujairah.

His approach transcends traditional safety management. Shanavas combines forensic incident investigation, predictive risk assessment, and crisis-ready emergency planning with a deep understanding of human psychology—recognizing that the safest workplaces are built on behavioral insight, not just regulations.

A distinguishing strength lies in his excellence in HSE documentation and digital systems mastery. Throughout his career, Shanavas has specialized in creating comprehensive safety documentation, from incident reports and risk assessments to compliance records and emergency action plans. His advanced computer proficiency enables him to streamline documentation processes, implement digital HSE management systems, and maintain meticulous records that stand up to the most rigorous audits. This technical aptitude, combined with attention to detail, makes him exceptionally effective in both field operations and administrative excellence.

Armed with a Bachelor's in Health and Safety Management, NEBOSH International Diploma, and certifications in psychological assessment, Shanavas bridges the gap between technical compliance and human-centered safety culture. His work spans high-stakes environments: industrial plants operating at capacity, real estate safety and security projects, and critical infrastructure where there's zero margin for error.
